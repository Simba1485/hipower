Growth Drivers

Why LPG Matters

01

Urbanization

Rapid urban expansion drives demand for clean cooking fuel. Pakistan's urban population grows at 2.7% annually, creating consistent LPG demand growth.

02

Gas Shortage

Natural gas supply constraints in winter months create significant LPG demand spikes. Many households rely on LPG as primary or backup fuel.

03

Industrial Growth

Manufacturing and commercial sectors increasingly adopt LPG for consistent energy supply, especially in areas with unreliable gas infrastructure.

04

Clean Energy Push

Government initiatives promote LPG as cleaner alternative to wood and biomass, supporting rural energy transition and environmental goals.
